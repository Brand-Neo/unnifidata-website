About Unnifi Device

80 Countries

Unnifi is a mobile wireless router that provides Wi-Fi services in 80 countries

E-SIM Card

The device does not require the installation of any physical SIM card

10 Hours

Unnifi has a built-in battery pack supplying up to 10 hours of continuous use

5 Devices

The device allows simultaneous connections of up to 5 devices

Device Functionality

  • 1 Wi-Fi LED indicator
  • 2 Signal LED indicator
  • 3 Battery LED indicator
  • 4 Strength LED indicator
  • 5 Micro USB (Input)
  • 6 Handle position for opening front panel
  • 7 Power button
  • 8 Reset button

Activating Unnifi in 3 Steps

1 Switch on the device
2 Sit back and relax
3 Once connected, the WIFI Indicator will lite up

Status Indicators

LED Indicator Type Status Remarks
Wi-Fi LED Indicator On Ready for internet connection
Off No internet connection
Flashing Service connection in progress
Signal LED Indicator On The signal strength is shown by signal LED indicator
Flashing Service error
Battery LED Indicator On The battery capacity is shown by battery indicator
Off Charging

Connecting to Unnifi

1 Search for SSID “Unnifi_XXXX” and Password printed on the device.
2 Go to the WiFi settings of your personal device and select the above SSID and enter the password (case sensitive)
