About U-Data

Delivering technology solutions

Our company (UEN: 202009542C) was founded in 2017 as with the sole purpose of offering customers simple and secure access to high-speed mobile data that comes with no expiry. We have operated a variety of brands including; WeRocket, Unnitel and Your Wi-Fi which we continue to support. In 2020, our company was rebranded to better reflect our core intent as a data service provider.

We are ready
for the future

We are a proud Singaporean company. Our head office is registered in Singapore and it is where all staff is based. We have a dedicated support team, and we guarantee any customer contact will be addressed swiftly and professionally. This is our service guarantee.

Our strategic partner

We procure our data from uCloudlink (Nasdaq: UCL). Since 2014, UCL has demonstrated its innovation in the mobile technology arena to offer the world's leading mobile data traffic sharing marketplace based on Cloud SIM technology.

Our investor

Belt Road Capital Management has been with us since the beginning. They are a private equity investment manager who specialises in partnering with small and medium enterprises (SME’s) to support technical innovation, sustainability, and customer experience excellence.

Our leadership

With over 30 years of experience in delivering an exceptional customer experience in financial services, telecommunications and technology services, Maurice Barnes leads the U-Data team.
