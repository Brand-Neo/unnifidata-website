About U-Data

Delivering technology solutions

U-Data was founded in 2017 with the sole purpose of offering customers simple and secure access to high-speed mobile data that comes with no expiry. Our solution provides seamless data connectivity in over 100 countries for your devices backed by our Data Top-Up APP and customer support team based in Singapore.

We are ready for the future

We are a proud Singaporean company. Our head office is registered in Singapore and it is where all staff is based. We have a dedicated support team, and we guarantee any customer contact will be addressed swiftly and professionally. This is our service guarantee.
